About Ford Phillipines

Ford Group Philippines is the national sales and distribution subsidiary of Ford Motor Company in the United States that handles all sales, service, and marketing activities of Ford vehicles in the Philippines.
Ford offers its widest lineup of SUVs, trucks, and performance cars which includes the EcoSport, Everest, Explorer, Expedition, F-150, Ranger, Mustang and Territory.
Ford is committed to further improving the customer and ownership experience. To date, the Ford dealership network is present in 49 locations nationwide, facilitating sales and service transactions for its growing customer base.
Ford continues to make a difference in the communities where its employees work and live. Ford is an advocate of road safety with the globally-recognized Driving Skills for Life (DSFL) currently integrated into its corporate social responsibility programs. Through the Operation Better World initiative, Ford also contributes towards improving the lives of Gawad Kalinga communities and families by providing access to clean water and healthcare through the conduct of medical and dental missions and training of volunteers. Ford also sponsors students to technical schools and donates to disaster relief operations in times of need.
